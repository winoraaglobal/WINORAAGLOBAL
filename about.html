Our Story

From a simple idea to a full-spectrum production powerhouse, discover how Winoraa became a leader in creating unforgettable experiences.

2012

The Beginning

Our story began as a simple idea - that a fundamental shift is happening for the first time in history, where young people are placing more value on experiences than material things. A bold leap of faith was taken.

2013

First Event

We produced our first event in Houston, Texas. The Thunder Challenge Adventure Running Series launched with an unpredictable, terrain-based challenge that brought participants to incredible venues with secret obstacle courses.

2015

Recognition & Growth

Thunder Challenge was dubbed as one of the best obstacle and endurance races in the United States. We launched Festival Central, our flagship Music and Entertainment brand, establishing our presence in the music festival industry.

2018

Technology Innovation

Launched Winoraa Labs, focusing on developing people-centric technology at the core of our operations, and Winoraa Production Services to share our tools and knowledge with other event producers.

2025

Future Vision

Today, we continue to focus our creative energy on strategy, logistics, and execution behind powerful event brands, producing city-wide mega festivals, intimate concerts, and historic once-in-a-lifetime music events.
